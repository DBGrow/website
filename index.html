About Us

DBGrow was founded to build secure, automated, and intelligent tools for data management and scientific research. We want to help facilitate the growth of the Factom ecosystem by contributing open-source tools, server administration services, technical support, developer relations, and marketing services. We strongly believe that the open-source projects we are developing will help increase the utility, visibility, and value of Factom. The DBGrow team is very excited to be a part of the Factom Authority Set, and we take the commitment very seriously.
