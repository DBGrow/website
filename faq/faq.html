Can I cancel or upgrade at any time?

Absolutely! The instant you cancel you'll stop being charged for your database's resources.
How many free/paid databases can I have?

You can create as many you'd like!
Can I have multiple databases on my Shared, Standalone, or Replica Set instance?

Unfortunately, DBGrow currently supports one database per instance.
Why does my virtual RAM show a value higher than the total RAM of my instance?

This is a quirk of MongoDB itself. Virtual RAM represents the total size of your database's data files if they were paged into RAM. Sometimes fresh Standalone and Replica Set databases will pre-allocate data files up to several GB in size. You'll never be charged for this empty space, only the space your data occupies
The connection count in my monitoring tab shows connections I didn't make. What are these coming from?

These are connections made by our monitoring and management service that keep track of your database health! If you're running a Replica Set, these connections may also be from other members of your replica set needed to sync data.
When I add nodes I see down servers in my topology for a moment. Is this normal?

This is normal and has no effect on uptime! Both MongoDB and our monitoring service require a moment to orient the new instances within the Replica Set
I forgot the password for my admin database user. What do I do?

Please contact support and we'll reset your admin user's password.
Can I add another user besides the initial default one?

Absolutely. Your initial user comes with privileges to create new users on your database. You can use the db.createUser() and other user management methods to manage access natively for your friends or employees
I can't connect and am seeing errors about the SSL certificate not matching the hostname when connecting via SSL

To connect via SSL, please use the --sslAllowInvalidHostnames option or equivalent for your platform. DBGrow uses dynamic DNS to make sure that your connection URI points to your EC2 as they're upgraded or replaced over time. The connection code generator in your database console will generate a correct connection string in several popular languages.
Can I import my existing DB to DBGrow?

Yes! There are several ways depending on your requirements. Please see Importing a Database in our Docs for a tutorial.
How can I contact you?

You can get in touch with us any time by using the support chat button on the bottom right of your screen, or by emailing us at support@dbgrow.com
